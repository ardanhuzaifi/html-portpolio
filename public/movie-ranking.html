Top 3 anime of all time

One Piece

One Piece is an anime that tells the adventure of a pirate named Monkey D. Luffy and his crew as they search for the legendary treasure known as One Piece. Along their journey, they encounter various unique characters, face powerful enemies, and uncover mysteries within the vast and wondrous world. With Luffy's devil fruit powers and unwavering spirit, they sail across treacherous seas, confront extraordinary challenges, and aim to become the greatest Pirate King in the world.

Classroom of the Elite

The anime "Classroom of the Elite" is set in a prestigious high school called Koudo Ikusei. In this school, students are grouped based on their social class and measured by a strict point system. The story follows Ayanokouji Kiyotaka, an introverted student who joins Class D, considered the lowest class. However, Ayanokouji possesses exceptional intelligence and a dark secret. Along with the new friends he meets, he strives to uncover the secrets behind the ruthless school system and fight for their place amidst deadly competition.

Tensei Shitara Slime Datta Ken

"Tensei Shitara Slime Datta Ken" is an anime that tells the story of a man named Satoru Mikami who is suddenly killed and reincarnated as a slime in a fantasy world. In his new form, he gains various extraordinary abilities, including the power to take on any desired form. With a strong determination, he strives to build a fair and peaceful nation in the new world by gathering diverse allies, such as monsters and magical creatures. Through adventurous and action-packed battles, Satoru, now known as Rimuru Tempest, aims to achieve his goals and transform the world with his unique powers.
